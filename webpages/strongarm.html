Nell Strongarm's Crime Capsule spotted over Toledo

  • Updated: Nov. 10, 2025, 5:41 p.m.
  • |Published: Nov. 10, 2025, 5:08 p.m.
By
  • Anna Staver, cleveland.com

TOLEDO -- Residents of Toledo were panicked Monday by the appearance of Nell Strongarm's Crime Capsule hovering over downtown.

The capsule hovered over the city center from approximately 11 AM to 11:30, moving gradually from the area of Middlegrounds Metropark toward Maumee Bay. Colored beams from sensors utilizing unknown technology swept across buildings and vehicles, producing no visible harm, though officials urge residents to consult their doctor if any unexplained symptoms are seen.

The mayor and police commissioner have urged calm, but no motive has been established for the infamous villain's ominous behavior.

"I tried to drive Strongarm off, but unfortunately my limited powers of flight are no match for the technology of her powerful craft", said the city's defender, the Mud Hen. "I suspect that she is planning some sort of heist in cooperation with her sometimes accomplice, the Pittsburgh Stealer."

No further information was available at press time. Authorities urge residents with any information to contact the Toledo Police Department.
